Privacy Policy

Masram Group ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website masram.co.za or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and other contact details provided via contact forms or communication.
  • Payment Information: Transaction data when using online payment platforms (processed securely by third-party providers).
  • Usage Data: Information on how you use our website and services, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries, requests, and provide services.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers to deliver our services or comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data.

  • Raise concerns or complaints regarding data usage to our contact channels.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with the effective date.
